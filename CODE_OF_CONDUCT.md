# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and evidence, not personal characteristics.
- Give actionable, respectful feedback and accept corrections gracefully.
- Respect historical and cultural subjects without using them to target present-day people or groups.
- Credit sources and other contributors accurately.
- Avoid harassment, threats, sexualized attention, discrimination, doxxing, and sustained disruption.

## Scope and enforcement

This code applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other project spaces. Project maintainers may edit or remove content and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when conduct threatens the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer rather than opening a public issue. Reports will be reviewed in good faith and handled as confidentially as practical.
